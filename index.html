Our Mission:

To bring the Obama Campus, future site of the prestigious Obama presidental library, to Chicago's Bronzeville neighborhood. There it will not only honor a president who once called Chicago home, but bring an anchor of academic and economic growth to Chicago and the Bronzeville neighborhood.

Learn more about our vision, our coummnity, our plans for the campus, and consider donating to our goal.

About the Foundation

The Barack Obama Presidential Library and Museum Campus Foundation is a 501(c)3 organization formed by community leaders in Bronzeville. The group consists of influential leaders from around Bronzeville and has strong representation from the Black Metropolis National Heritage Area Commission (BMNHAC), a group that is seeking National Heritage Area status for the historical, neighborhood of Bronzeville.

The organization includes experts with a variety of skills sets at a local, regional and national level. The core team members and partners have been working with advisors throughout the Chicago region to compile a well-researched and inspiring package. The experts include local Bronzeville development and engineering partners, as well as nationally-recognized urban design and biomimicry experts.
